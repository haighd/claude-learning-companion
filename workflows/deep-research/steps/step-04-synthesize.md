# Step 4: Synthesize Conclusions

## Objective
Convert analysis into clear conclusions and actionable recommendations.

## Instructions

### 1. Answer the Core Question

Return to the research question from Step 1.

**Direct Answer:**
- State the answer clearly in 1-2 sentences
- Acknowledge uncertainty where it exists
- Note conditions that affect the answer

**Format:**
```markdown
## Conclusion

**Question:** [Original research question]

**Answer:** [Direct, clear answer]

**Confidence:** [high/medium/low]

**Conditions:** [When this answer applies/doesn't apply]
```

### 2. Formulate Recommendations

Based on findings, what actions should be taken?

**For each recommendation:**
- What: Specific action to take
- Why: Which findings support this
- When: Urgency/timing
- Risk: What could go wrong

**Recommendation Template:**
```markdown
### Recommendation [N]: [Action]

**Action:** [What to do]
**Rationale:** [Why, based on findings]
**Priority:** [high/medium/low]
**Risk:** [Potential downsides]
**Dependencies:** [What needs to happen first]
```

### 3. Identify Follow-up Work

What additional work might be needed?

- Deeper investigation into specific areas
- Validation through experimentation
- Expert consultation
- CEO decisions required

### 4. Extract Learnings

What was learned that applies beyond this research?

**Potential Heuristics:**
- Patterns that might become golden rules
- Approaches that worked well
- Pitfalls to avoid in future research

**Format:**
```markdown
### Potential Heuristic: [Rule]
- **Domain:** [Where it applies]
- **Confidence:** [How sure are we]
- **Source:** This research
```

---

## Quality Checklist

Before proceeding:
- [ ] Core question is directly answered
- [ ] Recommendations are specific and actionable
- [ ] Risks are acknowledged
- [ ] Follow-up work is identified
- [ ] Learnings are captured

---

## Decision Gate

**Options:**
- [C] Continue to Step 5 (Document)
- [V] Validate conclusions (test against criteria)
- [E] Escalate to CEO (decisions needed)
